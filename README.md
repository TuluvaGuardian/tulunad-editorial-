## 📢 Mission Statement
The **Tuluva Guardian** is a digital advocacy platform and editorial bureau dedicated to the linguistic rights, academic excellence, and cultural sovereignty of the Tulu-speaking regions. We provide high-level mandates on education policy, regional infrastructure, and the preservation of Tuluva heritage.

## ✍️ Current Editorial: The SSLC 525 Shift
This repository hosts the official response to the 2026 Karnataka SSLC aggregate restructuring. Our editorial highlights the **100% success rate** of Tulu-speaking taluks and demands the immediate elevation of Tulu to **First Language** status.

### 🎯 Core Demands
1. **Academic Recognition:** Elevation of Tulu as a 1st Language in high-performance Taluks.
2. **Policy Transparency:** Ensuring the 525-mark shift does not dilute regional linguistic studies.
3. **Linguistic Justice:** Direct recognition of the Classical Status of Tulu.
